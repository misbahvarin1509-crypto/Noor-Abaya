Our Story

Crafted with Purpose, Worn with Pride

From a single sewing table to a brand trusted by thousands of women — this is the story of Noor Abaya.

12+ Years of Craft

Since 2014

A Small Table, A Big Dream

Noor Abaya began in a small Karachi workshop, where our founder hand-stitched her first collection of ten abayas for family and friends. Word spread quickly — not because of clever marketing, but because of the way the fabric moved, the way the seams sat, the way each piece simply felt different.

Today, that same attention to detail guides every garment we make. We have grown into a full atelier, but we still cut, drape, and finish every abaya the way we did on day one: by hand, with patience, and without shortcuts.

Our promise remains unchanged — modest fashion should never mean compromising on quality, comfort, or elegance.

Milestones Along the Way

Noor Abaya opens as a one-room workshop with a single sewing machine and ten original designs.

2017

Our First Boutique

2020

Going Online

We launch our online store, bringing Noor Abaya to customers across the country and beyond.

2023

Worldwide Shipping

2026
